Success Guidelines

Cooking Guidelines

  • Max 2 tsp oil/day (gingelly, groundnut, or sunflower)
  • No sugar in tea/coffee - use stevia if needed
  • Steam, boil, or grill instead of deep frying
  • Use herbs and spices for flavor instead of excess salt

Activity Guidelines

  • Walk 30-40 minutes daily (morning or evening)
  • Light home workout or yoga (3-4 times/week)
  • Take stairs instead of elevator when possible
  • Stand and stretch every hour if sedentary

Lifestyle Guidelines

  • Sleep 7-8 hours daily for optimal metabolism
  • Drink plenty of water (3+ liters throughout day)
  • Manage stress through meditation or deep breathing
  • Eat mindfully without distractions

Success Tips

  • Consistency is key - follow for at least 4 weeks
  • Measure progress weekly, not daily
  • Take before/after photos for motivation
  • Celebrate non-scale victories like energy levels
